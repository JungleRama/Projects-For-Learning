Lion

A lion's roar can be heard up to 8 kilometers away.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera Leo
  • Average Length: 1.8 - 2.1 meters (male)
  • Average Lifespan: 10 - 14 years in the wild
  • Habitat: Grasslands, Savannas, and Woodlands

Known as the "king of the jungle," lions do not actually live in jungles but in grasslands and plains. Male lions are distinguished by their mane, which is a unique characteristic among felines. These social cats live in groups called prides, typically consisting of a few males, related females, and cubs. They are apex predators and play a crucial role in the ecosystem by helping to control herbivore populations.
